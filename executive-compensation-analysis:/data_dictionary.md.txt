# Data Dictionary

## Compensation Variables
- **salary**: Base salary of CEO (USD millions, inflation adjusted).
- **bonus**: Annual cash bonus linked to performance (USD millions).
- **stock_options**: Value of stock options granted (USD millions).
- **rsu**: Value of Restricted Stock Units granted (USD millions).
- **equity**: Total equity-based pay (stock options + RSUs).

## Performance Variables
- **roe**: Return on Equity (%).
- **eps**: Earnings per Share (USD).
- **stock_return**: Annual stock return (%).
- **revenue**: Firm revenue (USD millions).
- **revenue_growth**: Year-on-year revenue growth (%).

## Control Variables
- **firm_size**: Firm size (log of total assets).
- **leverage**: Debt-to-equity ratio.
- **industry_dummy**: Industry classification dummies.
- **ceo_tenure**: CEO tenure (years).
- **year**: Time dummy (year fixed effects).
